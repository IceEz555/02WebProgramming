Escaping to Pattaya: My Journey Through Sea, Food, and Sunshine

Apivit Ice

Trip Leader
June 15, 2025 10 min read 32 Comments

Overview

  • Day 1
  • Day 2
  • Day 3

Day 1

We kicked off the day with an early train ride out of the city. The slow, scenic journey gave us time to relax and soak in the countryside views. Around late morning, we arrived in Pattaya and headed to our first stop: Krua Ban Phor Ta, a cozy restaurant with delicious local dishes.

After lunch, we made our way to Monsters Aquarium Pattaya — a surprisingly fun experience where we got to feed fish, goats, and even take quirky photos with exotic animals. Definitely more entertaining than expected!

Later in the day, we checked in to our hotel for a quick rest. As sunset approached, we wandered along Jomtien Beach, feeling the cool breeze and watching the waves roll in. The day ended with a stroll through the night market, picking up street food and snacks to enjoy back at the room.

Relaxing journey from city to coast with great views

Monsters Aquarium

Beach & Market Walk

Every trip starts with a little chaos and a lot of excitement — and our journey to Pattaya was no different. From catching an early train to chasing sunsets at the beach, Day 1 was all about slowing down and soaking it all in.

“Sometimes the best journeys are the ones that start without a plan just good food, great friends, and ocean air.”

Me, probably after a grilled squid at the night market

Day 2 Pool Villa Chill

On the morning of Day 2, we checked out from the hotel and headed to Makro to shop for dinner ingredients. We spent about two hours browsing for snacks, drinks, and everything we needed for a cozy night at our new place.

By early afternoon, we hopped in a car and made our way to the villa. Once we arrived, the fun really began — we played pool, jumped into the swimming pool, and prepped for dinner together. The evening was filled with laughter, delicious food, and great company.

As night fell, we gathered around for board games and even braved a horror movie marathon before drifting off to sleep. It was a chill but memorable day, packed with small joys and shared moments.

Villa Activities

Pool games, dinner prep, and relaxing together

Night Fun

Board games, horror movies, and laughter till we fell asleep

Day 3 Sushi & City Stroll

We started Day 3 by packing up and checking out from the villa. Though a bit tired from the night before, we were still full of energy for one last stroll around Pattaya.

Our main destination for the day was Central Pattaya. We headed straight to Sushiro and enjoyed a delicious lunch filled with sushi plates zipping by on conveyor belts — everyone got their favorites!

After lunch, we took a walk around the mall, picked up some souvenirs, and soaked in the final bits of vacation energy before preparing to head home. It was the perfect slow-down day to end our little adventure.
